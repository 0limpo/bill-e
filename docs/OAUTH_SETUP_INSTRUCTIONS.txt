================================================================================
                    CONFIGURACION DE OAUTH PARA BILL-E
                    Google / Facebook / Microsoft
================================================================================

Necesitas crear una "app" en cada plataforma para obtener:
- Client ID (publico)
- Client Secret (privado)

Al final, agregaras estas variables en Render:
- GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID
- GOOGLE_CLIENT_SECRET
- FACEBOOK_CLIENT_ID
- FACEBOOK_CLIENT_SECRET
- MICROSOFT_CLIENT_ID
- MICROSOFT_CLIENT_SECRET

================================================================================
                            1. GOOGLE
================================================================================

1. Ve a: https://console.cloud.google.com/

2. Crea un proyecto nuevo (o usa uno existente):
   - Click en el selector de proyectos (arriba)
   - "New Project"
   - Nombre: "Bill-e" o similar
   - Click "Create"

3. Habilita la API de Google+:
   - Menu hamburguesa > "APIs & Services" > "Library"
   - Busca "Google+ API" o "Google Identity"
   - Click "Enable"

4. Configura la pantalla de consentimiento:
   - Menu > "APIs & Services" > "OAuth consent screen"
   - User Type: "External"
   - Click "Create"
   - Llena los campos:
     * App name: Bill-e
     * User support email: tu email
     * Developer contact: tu email
   - Click "Save and Continue"
   - Scopes: Click "Add or Remove Scopes"
     * Selecciona: email, profile, openid
   - Click "Save and Continue"
   - Test users: Agrega tu email para pruebas
   - Click "Save and Continue"

5. Crea las credenciales:
   - Menu > "APIs & Services" > "Credentials"
   - Click "+ Create Credentials" > "OAuth client ID"
   - Application type: "Web application"
   - Name: "Bill-e Web"
   - Authorized JavaScript origins:
     * http://localhost:3000
     * https://bill-e.vercel.app
   - Authorized redirect URIs:
     * http://localhost:3000/api/auth/callback/google
     * https://bill-e.vercel.app/api/auth/callback/google
     * https://bill-e-backend-lfwp.onrender.com/api/auth/google/callback
   - Click "Create"

6. Copia tus credenciales:
   - Client ID: xxxx.apps.googleusercontent.com
   - Client Secret: GOCSPX-xxxx

================================================================================
                            2. FACEBOOK
================================================================================

1. Ve a: https://developers.facebook.com/

2. Click "My Apps" (arriba derecha) > "Create App"

3. Selecciona el tipo de app:
   - "Consumer" o "None" (para autenticacion basica)
   - Click "Next"

4. Configura la app:
   - App name: Bill-e
   - App contact email: tu email
   - Click "Create App"

5. Agrega el producto "Facebook Login":
   - En el dashboard, busca "Facebook Login"
   - Click "Set Up"
   - Selecciona "Web"
   - Site URL: https://bill-e.vercel.app
   - Click "Save" y "Continue"

6. Configura Facebook Login:
   - Menu izquierdo > "Facebook Login" > "Settings"
   - Valid OAuth Redirect URIs:
     * http://localhost:3000/api/auth/callback/facebook
     * https://bill-e.vercel.app/api/auth/callback/facebook
     * https://bill-e-backend-lfwp.onrender.com/api/auth/facebook/callback
   - Click "Save Changes"

7. ObtÃ©n las credenciales:
   - Menu izquierdo > "Settings" > "Basic"
   - App ID: es tu FACEBOOK_CLIENT_ID
   - App Secret: Click "Show" > es tu FACEBOOK_CLIENT_SECRET

8. Pon la app en modo Live:
   - Arriba del dashboard hay un toggle "In Development"
   - Cambialo a "Live"
   - (Necesitaras agregar Privacy Policy URL, puede ser un link temporal)

================================================================================
                            3. MICROSOFT
================================================================================

1. Ve a: https://portal.azure.com/

2. Busca "App registrations" en la barra de busqueda
   - O ve a: Azure Active Directory > App registrations

3. Click "+ New registration"
   - Name: Bill-e
   - Supported account types:
     * "Accounts in any organizational directory and personal Microsoft accounts"
     * (Esta opcion permite Outlook, Hotmail, Xbox, etc.)
   - Redirect URI:
     * Platform: Web
     * URL: https://bill-e-backend-lfwp.onrender.com/api/auth/microsoft/callback
   - Click "Register"

4. Copia el Application (client) ID:
   - En la pagina "Overview"
   - Application (client) ID: es tu MICROSOFT_CLIENT_ID

5. Crea el Client Secret:
   - Menu izquierdo > "Certificates & secrets"
   - Click "+ New client secret"
   - Description: "Bill-e production"
   - Expires: 24 months (o lo que prefieras)
   - Click "Add"
   - COPIA EL VALUE INMEDIATAMENTE (solo se muestra una vez)
   - Ese es tu MICROSOFT_CLIENT_SECRET

6. Configura permisos (opcional pero recomendado):
   - Menu izquierdo > "API permissions"
   - Ya deberia tener "User.Read" por defecto
   - Si no, click "Add permission" > "Microsoft Graph" > "Delegated"
   - Selecciona: User.Read, email, profile, openid

7. Agrega mas Redirect URIs:
   - Menu izquierdo > "Authentication"
   - Click "Add URI" y agrega:
     * http://localhost:3000/api/auth/callback/microsoft
     * https://bill-e.vercel.app/api/auth/callback/microsoft

================================================================================
                    RESUMEN - VARIABLES PARA RENDER
================================================================================

Una vez que tengas todo, agrega estas variables en Render:

GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID=xxxxx.apps.googleusercontent.com
GOOGLE_CLIENT_SECRET=GOCSPX-xxxxx

FACEBOOK_CLIENT_ID=123456789
FACEBOOK_CLIENT_SECRET=abcdef123456

MICROSOFT_CLIENT_ID=xxxxxxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxxxxxxxxxx
MICROSOFT_CLIENT_SECRET=xxxxx~xxxxx

================================================================================
                            NOTAS IMPORTANTES
================================================================================

1. Los Redirect URIs deben coincidir EXACTAMENTE con lo que configures en el
   backend, incluyendo http vs https y trailing slashes.

2. Para desarrollo local, asegurate de agregar los URIs de localhost.

3. Google puede tardar unos minutos en propagar los cambios.

4. Facebook requiere que la app este en modo "Live" para que funcione con
   usuarios que no son testers.

5. Microsoft permite cuentas personales (Outlook/Hotmail) Y empresariales,
   dependiendo de la opcion que elegiste en "Supported account types".
